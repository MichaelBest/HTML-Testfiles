O Most Holy Lady Virgin Mary, do Thou save and preserve under Thy protection thy servant, Alexander. Cover him with Thy maternal garment, preserve him in the fear of God and in obedience to his parents. Entreat my Lord and Thy Son that He may give him that which is profitable for his salvation. I entrust him to Thy maternal care, for Thou art a Divine protection for Thy servants.
